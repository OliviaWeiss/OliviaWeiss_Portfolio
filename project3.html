Scarborough Shooting Stars

Design Statement

This project was created as a selfinitiated branding exercise in support of my application for the Scarborough Shooting Stars internship. I designed a gala invitation and a promotional email signature to demonstrate my ability to translate the team's brand into practical, real-world marketing assets. The designs prioritize brand consistency, clear visual hierarchy, and readability within email platforms, with careful attention to layout, spacing, and logo placement to ensure strong visual impact across different screen sizes and email clients.

Gala Invitation

Designed a formal digital invitation for the Scarborough Shooting Stars' Night of Stars Gala. The central star graphic is a stylized play on the team's logo, reinforcing brand recognition while supporting an elegant, event-focused layout optimized for digital promotion.

Email Signature

Designed a custom email signature for professional use and integrated a branded promotional banner to support the Scarborough Shooting Stars' Night of Stars Gala. The layout balances clear contact information with subtle event promotion, using brand aligned visuals and an email safe structure to ensure readability and consistency across platforms.
